Solving Richest Customer Wealth challenge

You are given an m x n integer grid accounts where accounts[i][j] is the amount of money the i​​​​​​​​​​​th​​​​ customer has in the j​​​​​​​​​​​th​​​​ bank. Return the wealth that the richest customer has. A customer's wealth is the amount of money they have in all their bank accounts. The richest customer is the customer that has the maximum wealth.
Example 1:
Input: accounts = [[1,2,3],[3,2,1]] Output: 6 Explanation: 1st customer has wealth = 1 + 2 + 3 = 6 2nd customer has wealth = 3 + 2 + 1 = 6 Both customers are considered the richest with a wealth of 6 each, so return 6.
Example 2:
Input: accounts = [[1,5],[7,3],[3,5]] Output: 10 Explanation: 1st customer has wealth = 6 2nd customer has wealth = 10 3rd customer has wealth = 8 The 2nd customer is the richest with a wealth of 10.
Constraints:
m == accounts.length n == accounts[i].length 1 <= m, n <= 50 1 <= accounts[i][j] <= 100
